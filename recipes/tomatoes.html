Fried Green Tomatoes!

Fried green tomatoes are a southern favorite! And the name of a great movie! Green tomatoes are an unripe tomato with much firmer flesh and a tart flavor. They are intentionally picked early specifically for this appetizer! They are typically fried in cormeal and served alone. This dish takes about 25 minutes to make.

Ingredients!

  • Eggs(2 Large Eggs)
  • Tomatoes(4 Large Green Tomatoes)
  • Milk(1/2 Cup)
  • Cornmeal(1/2 Cup)
  • Bread Crumbs(1/2 Cup)
  • Kosher Salt(2 Teaspoons Coarse Kosher Salt)
  • Black Pepper(1/4 Teaspoon)
  • Vegetable Oil(1 Qt Vegetable Oil for Frying)

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    3. Slice the tomatoes 1/2 inch thick. Discard the ends.

  3. Step 3

    4. Whisk together eggs and milk in a medium-sized bowl. Scoop flour onto a plate. Mix cornmeal, bread crumbs, salt, and pepper on another plate. Dip tomatoes into milk and egg mixture; dredge in breadcrumbs to completely coat.

  4. Step 4

    5. Heat oil in a large deep skillet to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Place tomatoes in hot oil in batches of 4 or 5, to prevent them from touching; fry until crisp and golden brown on one side, then flip and fry on other side.

  5. Step 5

    6. Transfer fried tomatoes to a paper towel-lined plate to drain. Repeat with remaining tomatoes.

  6. Step 6

    7. Enjoy!
