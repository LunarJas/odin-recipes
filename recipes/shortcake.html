Strawberry Shortcake

This super tasty dessert is perfect for a spring day! Strawberry shortcake is a dessert made with layers of shortcake, strawberries, and whipped cream. The macerated strawberries are usually left to sit in sugar for a while, to release their juice and combine with the sweet sugar.

This soft dessert's total time to make is an hour. Good thing about this recipe is that you may even have some of the ingredients in your pantry!

For this dessert, you will want an 8-inch round cake pan. This will feed up to eight people.

Ingredients!

  • Strawberries(6 Cups of Strawberries)
  • Sugar(1/3 Cup White Sugar)
  • Flour(2 1/4 Cups All Purpose Flour)
  • Baking Powder(1 Table Spoon)
  • Salt(1/4 Teaspoon)
  • Butter(1/2 Cup Cold Unsalted Butter, cut into 1/2-inch Pieces)
  • Egg(Lightly Beaten 1 Large Egg)
  • Half-and-Half(3/4 Cup)
  • Whipped Heavy Cream(1 1/2 Cup)
  • Vanillia(2 Teaspoons Vanilla Extract)
  • Powdered Sugar(1/4 Cup)

Steps on How to Make Strawberry Shortcake!

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather Ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    3. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Grease an 8-inch cake pan with butter and lightly dust with flour.

  3. Step 3

    4. Slice the strawberries and toss them with 1/3 cup of white sugar. Set aside.

  4. Step 4

    5. Combine flour, 1/4 cup of white sugar, baking powder, and salt in a medium bowl: cut in butter with a pastry blender or two knives until mixture resembles coarse crumble. Make a well i the center of mixture; add beaten egg, half-and-half, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla. Stir in mixture and no dry spots remain, being careful not to overmix.

  5. Step 5

    6. Spoon batter evenly into prepared cake pan.

  6. Step 6

    7. Bake in oven for 18 minutes. Insert toothpick into cake until it comes out clean and the top is golden brown. Let cool partially in cake pan for 10 minutes. Flip cake onto a clean work surface.

  7. Step 7

    8. Beat heavy cream, powdered sugar, and remaining teaspoon of vanilla with an electric mixer on medium-high speed until soft peaks form. Do this for 3 minutes. Set aside.

  8. Step 8

    9. Cut partially cooled cake in half horizontally, making 2 layers; set aside top layer. Drain reserved strawberries in a colander placed over a small bowl, resserving any juice. Brush strawberry juice evenly over top of bottom cake layer. Evenly arrange 1/2 of the drained strawberries over bottom layer of cake.

  9. Step 9

    10. Spread half of whipped cream evenly over strawberries; place top cake layer over whipped cream. Top with remaining strawberries and whip cream.

  10. Step 10

    11. Enjoy!
