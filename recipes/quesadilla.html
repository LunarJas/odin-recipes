Chicken Quesadilla

A quesadilla is a Mexican dish that consists of a tortilla filled with cheese and often other ingredients, such as meat and veggies. It is cooked on a griddle or stove. Full quesadillas feature cheese sandwiched between two flat tortillas. Half quesadillas, like the one in this recipe, is a single tortilla that has been topped with fillings and folded into a half moon shape.

Ingredients

  • Bonless Chicken Breast(One Pound, Diced)
  • Fajita Seasoning(One 1.27 oz Packet)
  • Vegetable Oil(1 Tablespoon)
  • Green Bell Peppers(2, Chopped)
  • Red Bell Peppers(2, Chopped)
  • Flour Tortilla(Ten 10-inch Tortillas)
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese(One 8 oz Package)
  • Shredded Monterey Jack Cheese(One 8 oz Package)
  • Bacon Bits(1 Tablespoon)

Steps to Take

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather ingredients, preheat the broiler, and lightly grease a baking sheet.

  2. Step 2

    3. Toss chicken with fajita seasoning, then spread onto the baking sheet. Place under the broiler and cook until chicken is cooked through and no longer pink in the center, about 5 minutes

  3. Step 3

    4. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)

  4. Step 4

    5. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Stir in bell peppers, onion, and broiled chicken. Cook and stir until veggies have softened for about 10 minutes.

  5. Step 5

    6. Layer half of each tortilla with chicken and vegetable mixture, chedder and monterey cheese, and bacon bits

  6. Step 6

    7. Fold Tortillas in half and place onto baking sheet.

  7. Step 7

    8. Bake quesadilla in oven until cheese is melted.

  8. Step 8

    9. Enjoy!
